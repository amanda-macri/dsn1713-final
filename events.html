PRIVATE EVENTS

At 3 Sisters, we offer exceptional catering for private events, bringing our delightful baked goods and savory meals to your gatherings. Whether it’s a birthday celebration, corporate meeting, or family reunion, our customizable menu features fresh paninis, gourmet soups, and sweet treats, ensuring a memorable culinary experience for you and your guests. Let us handle the details while you enjoy the festivities!

Three Sisters offers a unique setting for a variety of private events, such as:

  • Retirement Parties
  • Showers
  • Birthdays
  • Weddings
  • Book Signings
  • Anniversary Parties
  • Holiday Parties
  • and more!
